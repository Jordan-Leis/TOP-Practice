Mac n cheese

An amazing meal for any family

Macaroni and Cheese, often lovingly referred to as "mac n cheese," is the quintessential comfort food that transcends generations. This classic dish combines tender macaroni pasta with a luxuriously creamy cheese sauce, creating a symphony of savory flavors and irresistible indulgence. Whether enjoyed as a simple weeknight dinner, a side dish at gatherings, or a nostalgic treat from childhood, mac and cheese holds a special place in the hearts and stomachs of many.

Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni pasta
  • Cheddar cheese (or your favorite cheese variety)
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • All-purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Optional: Bread crumbs (for topping)
  • Optional: Paprika or mustard powder (for added flavor)

Directions

  1. Cook the elbow macaroni pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.
  3. Once the butter is melted, whisk in flour to create a roux. Cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly, until the mixture turns golden brown and fragrant.
  4. Gradually pour in milk while continuing to whisk, ensuring there are no lumps in the sauce.
  5. Cook the sauce over medium heat, stirring frequently, until it thickens and coats the back of a spoon, about 5-7 minutes.
  6. Reduce the heat to low and gradually add shredded cheese to the sauce, stirring until melted and smooth.
  7. Season the cheese sauce with salt and black pepper to taste. Optional: Add paprika or mustard powder for additional flavor.
  8. Add the cooked macaroni pasta to the cheese sauce and stir until evenly coated.
  9. If desired, transfer the macaroni and cheese mixture to a baking dish and sprinkle with breadcrumbs for a crispy topping.
  10. Bake the macaroni and cheese in a preheated oven at 350°F (175°C) for 20-25 minutes, or until bubbly and golden brown on top.
  11. Let the macaroni and cheese cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy your homemade comfort food!
