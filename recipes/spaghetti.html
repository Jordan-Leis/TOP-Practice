I LOVE THE Spaghetti

Spaghetti is a classic, an amazing italian classic that i love

Spaghetti is a timeless Italian classic that never fails to satisfy. With its long, slender strands of pasta enveloped in a rich, savory sauce, it's a comfort food staple cherished around the world. Whether topped with a hearty marinara sauce bursting with ripe tomatoes and aromatic herbs, or adorned with a creamy Alfredo sauce and sprinkled with Parmesan cheese, spaghetti offers endless possibilities for culinary creativity. Pair it with meatballs, grilled vegetables, or seafood for a wholesome and fulfilling meal that delights the taste buds and warms the soul. Easy to prepare and endlessly versatile, spaghetti is a beloved favorite that promises to bring joy to any table.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Optional: Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the spaghetti pasta to the boiling water and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  3. While the pasta is cooking, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add minced garlic and chopped onion to the skillet and saut√© until fragrant and translucent.
  5. If using ground beef or Italian sausage, add it to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through.
  6. Pour the tomato sauce into the skillet and season with Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper. Stir well to combine.
  7. Reduce the heat and let the sauce simmer for about 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  8. Once the spaghetti is cooked, drain it and add it to the skillet with the sauce.
  9. Toss the spaghetti with the sauce until evenly coated.
  10. Remove from heat and serve the spaghetti hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
